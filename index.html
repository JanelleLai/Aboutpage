I enjoy decorating my diary with stickers, little collages, and photos with my friends. It’s my way of keeping all my favorite memories in one place!
Making resin art was one of the first hobbies I discovered. It takes a lot of time just to make one tiny keychain, but the final result always makes me feel so fulfilled.
All the cute beaded jewelry on social media really inspired me, so I started making my own. Before moving to Canada, I actually ran a small business selling them.
Crocheting is such a comforting hobby for me. It’s relaxing, and time just slips by without me even noticing. I love working on a piece while listening to my favourite music on a warm afternoon. ^u^
I’ve been into drawing and painting ever since childhood. I’d love to take lessons to improve someday, but I haven’t been able to fit it into my schedule yet.
I’m the type who instantly takes out their camera whenever I spot a pretty view. Photography is special to me because it captures moments my eyes can’t save on their own.
